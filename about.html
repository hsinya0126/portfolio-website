Hello! I'm Hsinya 👋

I am a Strategic Designer and Researcher with 8 years of experience driving end-to-end innovation within complex global organizations. My work lives at the intersection of service design, UX research, and product strategy, where I specialize in translating intricate customer ecosystems into seamless, scalable digital experiences. ⭐️

Currently, I am a Service Design Senior Associate at JPMorgan & Chase. In this role, I lead journey mapping and systems-thinking initiatives to modernize global platforms and self-service experiences. My recent work on digital debit initiatives serves over 25 million cardholders and is projected to deliver over $5.6M in annual savings by optimizing high-impact customer touchpoints. 📈

Prior to joining the financial sector, I spent years at Walmart Global Tech and Meta, where I led mixed-methods research for B2B enterprise tools and social media creator experiences. Whether I am conducting generative studies to inform AI-powered workflows or facilitating design thinking workshops for hundreds of stakeholders, my goal remains the same: transforming business complexity into intuitive, human-centered solutions. 👩‍🔧

Beyond my corporate work, I am the Co-founder and Host of the Design Nomads Podcast. We have grown into the most popular UX design podcast in the Mandarin-speaking world, hitting #1 on Spotify in Taiwan and reaching over 400,000 downloads. By interviewing global design leaders across 60+ episodes, I strive to bring cutting-edge insights and mentorship to the next generation of design professionals. 🎙️

Hello! I'm Hsinya 👋

I am a mixed-methods UX researcher and strategist with 7 years of expertise spanning research, service design, and product strategy. Currently, as a Senior Design Researcher at Walmart Global Tech, I lead high-impact research initiatives for the enterprise B2B domain, leveraging emerging technologies to transform complex business challenges into intuitive human experiences. ⭐️

Within Walmart’s Risk Technology division, I collaborate with cross-functional leadership to define product and business strategies across Safety & Security, Global Responsibility, and Governance. My work directly impacts the digital experience for 2.1 million associates and over 100,000 global suppliers. By directing 12 strategic research initiatives, I have optimized end-to-end workflows that drive measurable growth in productivity, safety, and associate satisfaction. 👩‍🔧

Beyond the corporate space, I am the co-founder and host of the Design Nomads Podcast—the premier UX design podcast in the Mandarin-speaking world. Reaching #1 in the design category on Spotify (Taiwan), I have produced over 60 episodes featuring global UX leaders, delivering cutting-edge insights to a growing community of design professionals. 🎙️
